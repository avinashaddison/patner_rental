# Community & Safety Guidelines

**Companion Ranchi**
Effective date: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}

Companion Ranchi only works if everyone feels safe and respected. These
Guidelines are part of our [Terms of Service](terms-of-service.md). Breaking them
can lead to content removal, suspension, or a permanent ban, and may be reported
to the authorities.

## The five hard rules

### 1. 18+ only

Everyone on Companion Ranchi must be **at least 18 years old**. We verify date of
birth at sign-up and again during Companion KYC. Misrepresenting your age results
in a permanent ban.

### 2. Companionship only — never adult services

Companion Ranchi is for **social companionship**: coffee, meals, movies,
shopping, events, city tours, networking, and conversation. It is **not** an
escort, dating, or adult-services platform. Any solicitation of sexual, romantic-
for-hire, or otherwise adult or illegal services is strictly prohibited and will
be reported.

### 3. Public places only

All meetings must happen in **public venues** — malls, cafés, restaurants, parks,
public events, co-working spaces, hotel lobbies, and tourist spots. **Private
residences and hotel rooms are never allowed.** Do not agree to move a meeting to
a private place.

### 4. Respect and consent

Treat every Companion and customer with respect. **Harassment, abuse, coercion,
discrimination, or unwanted contact** is grounds for an immediate ban. "No" means
no — consent can be withdrawn at any time, and either party may end a meeting.

### 5. Payments stay on-platform

Pay only through the in-app Razorpay checkout. **Cash or off-platform payment
requests are not allowed** and are a common sign of a scam or prohibited activity.
Report anyone who asks you to pay outside the app.

## Staying safe at a meeting

- Meet only in **busy, public places**.
- Tell a friend where you are going and when.
- Keep your first meetings short.
- Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, leave.
- Use the in-app **SOS** button to alert our team and share your location, and
  call **112** in an emergency.

## Safety tools (and their limits)

- **Report** — flag a user or a booking for our team to review.
- **Block** — stop another user from contacting or booking you.
- **SOS** — send an urgent alert (with your location, if available) to our safety
  team during a booking.

These tools help, but they **do not replace the emergency services**. In a real
emergency, always call **112** first.

## What we do about violations

We review reports and SOS alerts and may warn, suspend, or permanently ban
accounts, remove content, cancel bookings, and refer serious matters to
law-enforcement. Companions found in breach may forfeit pending payouts where
permitted by law.
