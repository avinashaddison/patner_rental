This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Companion Ranchi (the "Platform")
collects, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and the choices you have.
It is written to align with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
(DPDP Act)**. By using the Platform you agree to this Policy.

## 1. Data we collect

**You give us:**

- **Account details:** mobile number, full name, gender, **date of birth**, city,
  and optionally an email address and profile photo.
- **Companion KYC (Companions only):** a government-issued ID image and a selfie,
  used to verify identity and age. This is **sensitive data** and is handled with
  additional safeguards (see Section 5).
- **Bookings & content:** activity, meeting place type and location text, dates,
  messages, and images you send in chat.
- **Support & safety:** the contents of support conversations, reports you file,
  and users you block.

**Collected automatically:**

- **Payments:** processed by **Razorpay**. We receive payment status and
  identifiers; we do **not** store your full card or bank details — Razorpay does,
  under its own privacy policy.
- **Device & usage:** technical data needed to run the service and a push
  notification token (if you enable notifications).
- **Location:** if you trigger an **SOS** alert, the app may attach your current
  GPS location so responders can assist you. Location is used for that purpose
  and is not tracked in the background.

## 2. How we use your data

We use your data to:

- Verify your eligibility (18+) and, for Companions, your identity.
- Operate the marketplace: discovery, bookings, chat, payments and payouts.
- Keep the community safe: KYC, content moderation, reports, blocks, SOS, and
  enforcement of our Guidelines.
- Send you booking, account, and safety notifications.
- Meet legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations.

We rely on your consent, the performance of our contract with you (the Terms),
and our legal obligations as the lawful bases for processing.

## 3. Who we share it with

We share the **minimum necessary** data with:

- **The other party to a booking** (a Companion or customer you book) — e.g. name
  and profile, so the meeting can happen.
- **Service providers** who process data on our behalf, such as **Razorpay**
  (payments), our cloud hosting and media-storage providers, and SMS/push
  providers — bound to use it only to provide their service.
- **Authorities**, when required by law or to protect the safety of users.

We **do not sell your personal data.**

## 4. Storage and international transfer

Data is stored with reputable cloud providers. Some providers may process data
outside India; where they do, we take steps consistent with the DPDP Act to
protect it.

## 5. KYC documents — special handling

Government IDs and selfies are collected only to verify Companions. We:

- store them privately and access them only through short-lived, authenticated
  links (not public URLs);
- use them only for verification, safety, and legal compliance;
- **delete them when they are no longer needed** — including when a verification
  is rejected/superseded and when an account is closed (subject to any retention
  the law requires).

## 6. Retention

We keep personal data for as long as your account is active and as long as needed
for the purposes above or to meet legal/accounting obligations. When you delete
your account we delete or anonymise your personal data, and we **delete your KYC
images**, except records we are legally required to retain (e.g. transaction
records for tax).

## 7. Your rights

Subject to the DPDP Act, you can:

- **Access and correct** your profile data in-app.
- **Block** users and manage notification preferences.
- **Withdraw consent** and **delete your account** — in-app where available, or
  by contacting us at {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}. Deleting your account removes your
  profile and KYC images as described in Section 6.
- **Nominate** another person to exercise your rights in case of death or
  incapacity, and **raise a grievance** (Section 9).

## 8. Children

The Platform is strictly for adults **18 and older**. We do not knowingly allow
or collect data from anyone under 18. If we learn an account belongs to a minor
we will close it and delete the data.
