# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Companion Ranchi**
Effective date: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}
Operated by: {{LEGAL_ENTITY_NAME}}

This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work on Companion Ranchi. It
forms part of, and should be read with, our
[Terms of Service](terms-of-service.md).

## 1. How payments work

You pay for a booking up front through **Razorpay**. The amount is held against
the booking and is released to the Companion's wallet **only after the meeting is
completed**. Companion Ranchi retains a platform commission on each completed
booking; the remainder is the Companion's payout. We never collect cash.

## 2. Cancellation by the customer

You can cancel a booking from the booking screen. **Unless a different policy is
shown to you at checkout**, the following applies, based on how long before the
scheduled start time you cancel:

| When you cancel | Refund |
| --- | --- |
| More than **24 hours** before start | **100%** (full refund) |
| Between **6 and 24 hours** before start | **50%** (the rest compensates the Companion for the reserved time) |
| Less than **6 hours** before start, or **no-show** | **No refund** |

## 3. Cancellation by the Companion

If a Companion cancels, or does not show up at the agreed public place and time,
you receive a **full refund** to your original payment method. You may also report
the Companion so our team can review their account.

## 4. Safety-related cancellations

If a booking is cancelled because a **safety rule was broken** — for example, a
request to meet in a private place, a request for a prohibited (e.g. adult)
activity, harassment, or an attempt to move payment off-platform — then:

- the **user at fault is not entitled to a refund**, and
- their account may be **suspended or banned**.

If you ever feel unsafe, use the in-app **SOS** or **Report** tools, and contact
the police on **112** in an emergency.

## 5. How refunds are paid

Approved refunds are returned to your **original Razorpay payment method**.
Depending on your bank or card network, it usually takes **5–7 business days** for
the amount to appear. Our Support team can confirm the status at any time.

## 6. Disputes

If something went wrong with a booking, contact us through **in-app Support
within 48 hours** of the scheduled time. Our team can review the booking — chat,
timing, and status — and, where fair, issue a full or partial refund. All
payments on the Platform are reversible by Companion Ranchi admin.
